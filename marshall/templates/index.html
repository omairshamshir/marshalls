About us

Our goal is to work collaboratively with our clients, providing comprehensive advisory services for enforcement of their legal rights and development of their businesses in United Kingdom and overseas. Our commitment is to assist our clients on the basis of in-depth knowledge of their field activities, in addition to legal requirements relating to their matters. Advisors at Edward Marshall Solicitors have experience in multiple enterprises relating to various practice areas and thus making them the best in their relevant fields. We have provided advisory services to clients and obtained positive results in a large number of immigration matters, corporate disputes, employment proceedings, family law and property matters. Edward Marshall Solicitors maintains outstanding professional relationships with local authorities and government agencies in the country, as well as with some of the international institutions which helps us deal with our clients' matters associated with such offices much expeditiously
